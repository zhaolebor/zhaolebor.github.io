About 13

"13" is a website created by a high school student who is very interested in mystery movies. This site creates a platform for mystery movie fans to comment and recommend their favorite mystery movies. The number "13" means that there are always 13 movies displayed on the home page, and the users can decide which movies to be displayed by their votes.
